Period Panties

Period panties are reusable underwear with built-in absorbent layers to catch menstrual flow. They can be worn alone or as a backup with pads, tampons, or menstrual cups. Designed for comfort and leak protection, they provide a sustainable alternative to disposable menstrual products.

How to Use Period Panties?

  1. Choose the right absorbency level: Light, moderate, or heavy flow options are available.
  2. Wear like regular underwear: No additional steps are needed.
  3. Change as needed: Typically lasts 4 to 12 hours, depending on flow.
  4. Wash after use:
    • Rinse with cold water first.
    • Machine wash or hand wash with mild detergent.
    • Air dry to maintain absorbency and durability.

Pros & Cons of Period Panties

  • ✔ Comfortable and leak-proof: Designed for all-day wear.
  • ✔ Reusable and eco-friendly: Reduces waste compared to disposable products.
  • ✖ Requires proper washing and drying: Needs care to maintain absorbency.
  • ✖ Higher initial cost: More expensive than disposables, but cost-effective over time.
