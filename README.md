Femcare is an all-in-one period tracker and women’s wellness platform committed to breaking menstrual taboos and promoting open conversations around reproductive health. Our intuitive app allows users to track their cycles, log symptoms, and receive personalized insights. With an inbuilt AI-powered chatbot, Femcare offers instant support and reliable information anytime, anywhere.

Designed with inclusivity and empowerment in mind, Femcare focuses on key areas like menstruation, pregnancy, mental well-being, and nutrition. We aim to create a supportive space where women can take charge of their health confidently, connect with others, and access trusted resources—all in one place.

Join Femcare and be part of the movement to normalize period talk and celebrate the strength of the human body.
